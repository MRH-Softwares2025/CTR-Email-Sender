Need a Gmail App Password?

To use SMTP with Gmail, create a Google App Password and enter it here instead of your normal login password.

  1. Open Google Account Security.
  2. Enable 2-Step Verification if it is not already enabled.
  3. Click App passwords under Signing in to Google.
  4. Select Mail as the app and choose Other to label it.
  5. Copy the 16-character password and paste it here.

Use this app password for SMTP; do not use your normal Gmail password.
